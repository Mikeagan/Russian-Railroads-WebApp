Game Setup

Set the game up for 2 players. The Turn Order and Starting Bonus cards as well as the Last Round marker are not required.
  • Take 5 instead of just one coin
  • Give Emil a supply of 6 workers
  • Put 2 additional workers for Emil next to the 50 space of the score track
The field Take 2 Coins is not available in the solo game.

Game Sequence

At the beginning of a round, the application will ask you for the costs of the public engineer (1 or 2 workers or 1 coin). In later rounds you will also be asked about your points. If they are greater 50 or 150, Emil will get another worker each. This will usually happen between rounds in the scoring phase.

Emil will always start the round and wants to take an action. Which action it is will be shown by the application including the costs of it. You must decide whether Emil can take the action or not. In case he can, put the according workers or a coin from the general supply on that field and make your turn.

In case you accepted or rejected the action by mistake, you can remove it from the discard pile list by pressing clear or check next to the name in the list.

The game ends after 6 (or 5 rounds if you play with the coal module). All workers available to Emil, the number of drawable cards, remaining rounds and your points are displayed in the toolbar.

Notes

  • Emil does not need any many. He simply takes it from the general supply.
  • In case Emil don't have enough workers he will pass.
  • As soon as you reach 50+ or 150+ points, Emil will get another worker which he is allowed to use immediately (as long he has not passed yet).
  • Emil collects locomotives and engineers. He always takes the locomotive with the lowest number. If no locomotives are available anymore he will take the factory with the lowest number.
  • The number of engineers gathered by Emil are only required to check the majority of engineers for the according points.
